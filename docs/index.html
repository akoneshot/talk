Powerful features designed for professionals who value their time and privacy.

Local Whisper Transcription

OpenAI's Whisper runs entirely on your Mac with Metal acceleration. No internet required, no data sent anywhere.

AI Text Enhancement

Choose between local Ollama or cloud AI (Claude, OpenAI) to polish grammar, add punctuation, and structure your text.

Two Processing Modes

Simple: Quick filler word removal. Advanced: Full LLM-powered enhancement with smart formatting.

Global Hotkey

Press your custom hotkey from any app to start recording. Choose toggle mode or push-to-talk—whatever fits your flow.

Auto-Paste Anywhere

Transcribed text automatically pastes wherever your cursor is. Works with any app—emails, documents, messages, code editors.

Multiple Whisper Models

Choose from Tiny to Medium models. Balance speed vs. accuracy for your needs—from quick notes to detailed transcriptions.

Your voice stays yours

In a world of cloud-first solutions, DictAI takes a different approach. Your audio is processed entirely on your Mac using Whisper's local models. No servers, no uploads, no compromises on privacy.

Even AI enhancement can be fully local with Ollama—giving you the power of LLMs without sending a single word to the cloud.
